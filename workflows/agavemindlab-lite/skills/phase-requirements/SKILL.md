# Requirements

Goal: make the requested outcome clear enough to design or implement without guessing.

Do:
- Read the issue, comments, attachments, and previous artifacts.
- Identify the user-visible problem, scope, acceptance checks, and blockers.
- If a decision is required, ask it in the `## Requirements` artifact with a visible `### NEEDS CLARIFICATION` block; keep the question expanded.
- Otherwise publish `## Requirements` with conclusion, evidence, acceptance checks, and material risks.

Exit after posting the artifact. Do not implement in this phase.
